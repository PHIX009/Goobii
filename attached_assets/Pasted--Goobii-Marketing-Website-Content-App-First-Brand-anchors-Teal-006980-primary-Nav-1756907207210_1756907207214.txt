## SERVICES

**Intro (Sooftwash™)**
Sooftwash™ is our water‑saving method tailored for Dubai. Using just **2–4 L** of water and biodegradable, vehicle‑safe chemistry, we lift dust, sand, and road film effectively—protecting finishes and the environment.

**Service Cards (no prices)**

**Aura (Exterior)**
*Weekly upkeep made easy.* A focused exterior clean that removes dust, light road film, and everyday grime so your car stays consistently presentable between deeper washes. *Ideal cadence: weekly.*

**Aura Absolute (Exterior + Basic Interior)**
Everything in Aura plus a light interior refresh—quick vacuum, wipe‑downs, and glass—so the cabin feels as tidy as the exterior. *Great for routine maintenance.*

**Glow (Premium Exterior)**
Our more thorough exterior service. Extra attention to trims, badges, crevices, wheels and tyres for a sharper finish. **Recommended experience** if you haven’t cleaned in a while or want that extra care. *Takes a bit longer.*

**Glow Absolute (Full Premium)**
Glow exterior detail plus a more complete interior treatment: more surfaces addressed, in/out glass, and tighter nooks. *For an elevated, all‑round result.*

> **Time note:** Duration varies by vehicle size and condition. Your Liink will confirm on arrival.

## FAQs

**Service & Booking**
**Q:** How do I book a wash?
**A:** Download the app, choose a service (Aura/Aura Absolute/Glow/Glow Absolute), pick a time and location, and confirm.

**Q:** How long does a wash take?
**A:** It varies by vehicle size and condition. Your Liink will confirm on arrival.

**Q:** Do I need to be present?
**A:** Not always. Provide access instructions in the app and ensure the car is unlocked if interior work is included.

**Pricing & Payments**
**Q:** Where can I see prices?
**A:** Pricing is shown in the app before you confirm. Subscriptions are on a separate page without prices.

**Q:** What payment methods are accepted?
**A:** Major cards and digital payments (details shown in app at checkout).

**Environmental Impact**
**Q:** How much water do you save?
**A:** Sooftwash™ uses **2–4 L** per wash compared to **100–150 L** for pressure washes—saving tens of liters each time.

**Q:** Are your products safe?
**A:** Yes—biodegradable formulas chosen to be worker‑safe and vehicle‑safe.

**Quality & Guarantees**
**Q:** What if I’m not satisfied?
**A:** Tell us via the app within 24 hours; we’ll review and make it right.

**Q:** Are you insured?
**A:** We operate to professional standards and handle issues transparently. Coverage details are available upon request.

**App & Subscriptions**
**Q:** Can I request before/after photos?
**A:** Yes—toggle the option during booking.

**Q:** How do subscriptions work?
**A:** Choose Spark 4/8 or Flow 12 in the app, select Aura/Aura Absolute per visit, schedule, and manage. Pause or adjust anytime (T\&Cs apply).

**Service Areas**
**Q:** Where do you operate?
**A:** Dubai to start. Check the app for your specific area.
