# Goobii Marketing Website - Content Development Brief

## Project Overview
Goobii is an eco-friendly car cleaning service in Dubai that uses innovative Sooftwash™ technology. The company uses only 2-4 liters of water per wash compared to traditional methods that use 100-150 liters. This marketing website aims to drive app downloads and promote their sustainable car cleaning services.

## Brand Identity
- **Primary Color**: Teal (#006980) - used for all text and main branding
- **Secondary Color**: Navy Blue (#003B5C) - used for accents and highlights
- **Accent Color**: Orange (#FF8200) - used for active navigation and highlights
- **Background**: Light gray-blue (#ecf1f4)
- **Logo**: Vertical Goobii logo with teal coloring
- **Tone**: Professional, eco-conscious, innovative, accessible

## Content Development Guidelines

### Writing Style
- Clear, conversational tone
- Focus on benefits over features
- Use active voice
- Include specific numbers and statistics
- Address customer pain points
- Highlight unique value propositions

### Call-to-Action Strategy
- Primary CTA: App download
- Secondary CTAs: Service booking, learn more
- Use action-oriented language
- Create urgency where appropriate

### Local Market Considerations
- Dubai-specific content
- Regional preferences and needs
- Local regulations and standards
- Cultural sensitivity
- Climate-specific benefits (heat, dust)

## Technical Implementation Notes
